THE ART OF THE SLOW STITCH

At MASIB Collection, craftsmanship isn’t just a step in production — it’s a philosophy. Each pair of shoes is born from a careful blend of traditional artistry and modern precision.

From hand-cut premium leather to detailed stitching, our artisans dedicate hours to ensure every product meets the highest standards of comfort and elegance. We respect the natural tempo of creation, ensuring that West African craftsmanship lives in every stitch.

Pillars of Excellence

Our commitment to quality is built upon four fundamental principles that define every MASIB creation.

Ethical Materials

We use only the finest top-grain leathers, ethically sourced from local tanneries that practice sustainable tanning methods.

Precision Design

Every pattern is digitally optimized for comfort before being hand-transferred to leather for cutting by master artisans.

Ergonomic Flow

Our unique footbed architecture uses a blend of cork and memory foam to mold to your specific gait over time.

Heritage Skills

We invest in the next generation of shoemakers, keeping traditional Kano leatherworking techniques alive and thriving.

The Material Heritage

The soul of a MASIB shoe lies in its skin. We work primarily with vegetable-tanned leathers that develop a rich patina over time, telling the story of your journeys.

By avoiding harsh chemical dyes, we preserve the natural grain and breathability of the leather, ensuring your feet stay cool and comfortable in any climate.

The Journey of a Pair

Every MASIB creation undergoes a rigorous 72-hour transformation from raw hide to wearable masterpiece.

01

Sculpting the Last

We begin by drafting the silhouette, ensuring the anatomical last provides perfect arch support and a natural gait.

02

Precision Cutting

Using only prime sections of the leather, master cutters hand-carve each component to avoid natural stretching.

03

Hand Stitching

Artisans use reinforced wax thread to hand-stitch the upper, ensuring seams that are as durable as they are beautiful.

04

Bespoke Finishing

Finally, we hand-burnish the edges with organic oils to create a deep, lustrous patina unique to every pair.
