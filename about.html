OUR STORY

MASIB Collection was founded with a passion for artistry, precision, and individuality. Each piece is more than footwear — it’s a form of expression.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with innovative design, creating timeless shoes that deliver elegance, comfort, and durability in every step. Our skilled artisans work with premium materials, shaping each pair to perfection. From sketch to sole, every MASIB creation carries a story of dedication, detail, and purpose.

The Masib Philosophy

Defining the soul of artisanal luxury through four fundamental pillars of our house.

The Vision

To be the premier destination for West African luxury footwear, recognized globally for our commitment to heritage and modern aesthetics.

The Mission

Empowering local artisans while providing our clients with footwear that feels as good as it looks, intended for those who walk their own path.

Timelessness

We reject fast fashion. Every design is iterated to ensure it remains stylish today, tomorrow, and a decade from now.

Community

By producing locally in Kano, we support a thriving ecosystem of leatherworkers, tanners, and designers.

Meet the Founder

The vision and passion behind Masib Collection.

Masroor Ibrahim

Masroor Ibrahim is the visionary founder and master shoemaker behind Masib Collection. With a deep-rooted passion for traditional craftsmanship and an eye for modern aesthetics, he established the brand in 2021. His dedication to quality, ethical sourcing, and empowering local artisans drives the philosophy of every Masib creation.

— Founder & Master Shoemaker
