Our Essence

Masib Collection was born from a passion for traditional shoemaking and timeless design.

Preserving the Art of Handmade Shoes

Founded in 2021 by master shoemaker Masroor Ibrahim, our atelier in Kano, Nigeria brings together generations of shoemaking expertise. Each pair of Masib shoes represents over 200 meticulous steps performed by skilled artisans.

Our process respects the natural tempo of creation, ensuring that the legacy of West African craftsmanship lives in every stitch.
